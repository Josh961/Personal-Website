About Me

Hi, my name is Josh and I'm currently working as a software engineer building APIs and web applications within the healthcare space. I graduated from The University of Pittsburgh with a bachelor's in Computer Science and a certificate in Russian and East European Studies.

One which requires consistent improvement and making plenty of mistakes!
